1. Introduction

Welcome to HolyDham ("we," "our," or "us"). We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

By using HolyDham, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Information: When you use our contact form, you may provide your name, email address, subject, and message content.
  • Newsletter Subscription: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.
  • Account Information: If you create an admin account, we collect your username, email, and password (stored securely using one-way hashing).
  • User-Generated Content: Any content you submit, including images, descriptions, or comments.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and navigation paths.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring URLs, and pages viewed.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience and maintain session state.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To operate, maintain, and improve the HolyDham platform and its features.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send newsletter updates (if subscribed), and provide customer support.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our platform, identify popular content, and improve user experience.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or unauthorized access.
  • Content Curation: To personalize and improve the spiritual content we present based on aggregated usage patterns.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We deeply value your trust and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in limited circumstances: with service providers bound by confidentiality, when required by law, to protect safety, or with your explicit consent.

5. Cookies & Tracking

HolyDham uses essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from third-party advertisers.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including secure password hashing, HTTPS encryption, regular security audits, access controls, and encrypted backups.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months.

8. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete, object to processing, port, or withdraw consent for your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
